About

One Place for Every Stationery Need

Tharu Communication is your one-stop destination for books, stationery, and essential services, combining the warmth of a local shop with the convenience of modern e-commerce.

Opened in September 2024, Tharu Communication is based in Otthakade, Kahapathwala. Shop online or visit us for fast, affordable service and trusted products.
